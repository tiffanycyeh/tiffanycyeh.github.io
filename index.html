About me

Hello! I am currently a Product Designer for the Dealer Connectivity Data Services team at Ford Motor Company. I aspire to create and contribute to designing intuitive, efficient, and data-driven products that will inspire others to work smarter and faster in the information era that we live in today.

I have always been fascinated with technology as well as art. Since I was young, I have always desired to be involved in anything that would allow my creativity and vision to thrive, while tackling complex technical challenges. I find passion in utilizing data-driven technology, and finding opportunities for efficient yet artistic solutions. Always striving to learn new things, I graduated in 2016 from the School of Informatics & Computing from Indiana University Bloomington in 2016 and have been aiming to diversify and amplify my career within Ford IT ever since. In my free time during the 2020 pandemic, I enrolled in Northwestern the Data Science & Visualization Boot Camp, and am excited to implement my newfound knowledge into business practice.
